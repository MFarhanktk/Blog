Interesting Facts about HRM

  • HRM is not just about hiring and firing—it's a strategic partner in organizational success.
  • Google uses data-driven HR practices (called "People Analytics") to improve employee satisfaction.
  • Soft skills, such as emotional intelligence, are becoming just as important as technical skills in hiring decisions.
  • The term “Human Resources” was first used in the 1890s but became popular in the 1960s.
  • Remote work and hybrid models have transformed HRM approaches worldwide.
